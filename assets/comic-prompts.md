## Style Notes

- **Consistent palette:** Dark backgrounds, neon green (#00ff88) as primary accent, amber (#ffb800) for warnings/contrast
- **Typography in images:** Use bold, condensed fonts that match terminal/monospace aesthetic
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9 landscape (1024×576 minimum, 1920×1080 preferred)
- **File naming:** `slide-00-title.png`, `slide-01-problem.png`, ..., `slide-13-getstarted.png`
- **Style consistency:** All images should feel like they're from the same graphic novel — unified line weight, color treatment, and character design
